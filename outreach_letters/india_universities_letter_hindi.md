### Why each matters:
1. **IIT Bombay** — C-MInDS AI center. NVIDIA/Google-funded labs. #1-2 in India for CS.
2. **IIT Delhi** — School of AI (ScAI, est. 2020). Deep learning, NLP, reinforcement learning.
3. **IIT Madras** — RBCDSAI (Bosch-funded). #1 NIRF. Graph neural networks, healthcare AI. First AI BS degree.
4. **IISc Bangalore** — India's top research university. Oldest CS dept. Near Microsoft Research India.
5. **IIT Kanpur** — Historical CS powerhouse. C3iHub cybersecurity. Theoretical CS, ML, vision.
6. **IIT Kharagpur** — India's oldest IIT. Centre for AI. Indian-language NLP, data mining.
7. **IIT Hyderabad** — First IIT with separate AI department (BTech AI, 2019). Speech, healthcare AI.
8. **IIIT Hyderabad** — India's strongest for AI research. CVIT (world-class vision). LTRC (NLP). Kohli Centre.
9. **IIT Roorkee** — Asia's oldest tech institution (1847). AI for infrastructure, remote sensing.
10. **IIT Guwahati** — Mehta Family Centre for AI. NE Indian language tech, deep learning.
